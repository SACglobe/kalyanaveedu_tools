### ✅ Task 5 — Set Up Google Search Console
**Time needed:** 20 minutes
**Why:** Signals site ownership to Google. Required trust signal.

Steps:
1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Add Property"
4. Enter: kalyanaveedu.in
5. Choose verification method: "HTML tag" (easiest for WordPress)
6. Copy the meta tag Google gives you
7. In WordPress: go to a plugin like "Rank Math" or "Yoast SEO"
   → Webmaster Tools → paste the Google verification code
8. Click "Verify" in Search Console
9. Once verified, click "Sitemaps" → enter: sitemap.xml → Submit
10. Check "Coverage" tab for any crawl errors

**Done when:** Search Console shows "Ownership verified" ✓
