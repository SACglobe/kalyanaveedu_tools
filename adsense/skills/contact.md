## Why a Visible Email Address Matters

Your current contact page has only a form — no visible email address.
Google's reviewers cannot verify a contact form is real or working.
A visible email address (even Gmail) proves a real person can be reached.
This is one of the specific things they check.

If you are worried about spam: use a dedicated Gmail address for this site.
It does not need to be your personal email.
